    \item How many two-letter ``words'' start with either A or B? (In this case a ``word'' is just a sequence of letters; it doesn't have to be English, or even pronounceable. For instance ``XJ'' is a word.)

    \hfill Sum \hfill Product \hfill \phantom{.}

    Why? \vfill
    \item Your wardrobe consists of 2 shirts, 4 pants, and 18 bow ties. How many different outfits?

    \hfill Sum \hfill Product \hfill \phantom{.}
    
    Why? \vfill
    
    \item Suppose you roll a 12-sided die seven times, recording the number that appears after each roll. How many sequences of seven rolls are possible?

    \hfill Sum \hfill Product \hfill \phantom{.}
    
    Why? \vfill
    \item How many lattice paths from $(0, 0)$ to $(10,10)$ pass through the point $(4, 7)$?
    
    \hfill Sum \hfill Product \hfill \phantom{.}
    
    Why? \vfill
    \item How many different pizzas can you make if you can choose from 10 toppings and you can have any number of toppings on your pizza?
    
    \hfill Sum \hfill Product \hfill \phantom{.}
    
    Why? \vfill
    \item How many two-letter words start with one of the 5 vowels?

    \hfill Sum \hfill Product \hfill \phantom{.}

    Why? \vfill
    \item How many license plates can you make out of three letters followed by three numerical digits?

    \hfill Sum \hfill Product \hfill \phantom{.}

    Why? \vfill
    \item How many 10-bit strings of weight 6 start with either 11 or 00?

    \hfill Sum \hfill Product \hfill \phantom{.}

    Why? \vfill
\end{enumerate}
